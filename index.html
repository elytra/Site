Current mods

Correlated Maintained by unascribed Storage and automation for the modern world.

Store seemingly limitless amounts of items in a small space, and later retrieve them through a unified and searchable interface.

Davincis Vessels Maintained by Darkevilmac A minecraft mod that lets you fly a house.

Build things and put helms on them, then they can move. Sometimes it crashes your game.

Engination Maintained by Falkreon Cosmetic blocks and mechanical amusements for creative builds.

Currently just a large collection of (very pretty) decorative blocks. Maybe someday it'll have lazors. And industrialism. And automation.

Lingering Loot Maintained by capitalthree Custom dropped item despawn times for Forge.

A simple mod for adjusting the despawn time of different kinds of items, to decrease server load or just make it easier to get rid of junk items, or even make it harder to lose important items.
